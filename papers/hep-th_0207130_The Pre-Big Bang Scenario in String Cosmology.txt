Title: The Pre-Big Bang Scenario in String Cosmology
Authors: M. Gasperini, G. Veneziano
Published: 2002-07-12
arXiv ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/hep-th/0207130v1
Categories: hep-th, astro-ph, gr-qc, hep-ph
Abstract:
We review physical motivations, phenomenological consequences, and open problems of the so-called pre-big bang scenario in superstring cosmology.

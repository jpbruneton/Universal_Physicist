Title: Dark Energy
Authors: Miao Li, Xiao-Dong Li, Shuang Wang, Yi Wang
Published: 2011-03-30
arXiv ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/1103.5870v6
Categories: astro-ph.CO, gr-qc, hep-th
Abstract:
We review the problem of dark energy, including a survey of theoretical models and some aspects of numerical studies.

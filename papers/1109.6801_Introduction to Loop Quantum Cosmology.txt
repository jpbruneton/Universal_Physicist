Title: Introduction to Loop Quantum Cosmology
Authors: Kinjal Banerjee, Gianluca Calcagni, Mercedes Martín-Benito
Published: 2011-09-30
arXiv ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/1109.6801v2
Categories: gr-qc, astro-ph.CO, hep-th
Abstract:
This is an introduction to loop quantum cosmology (LQC) reviewing mini- and midisuperspace models as well as homogeneous and inhomogeneous effective dynamics.

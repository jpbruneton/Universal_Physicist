Title: Graviton propagator in loop quantum gravity
Authors: Eugenio Bianchi, Leonardo Modesto, Carlo Rovelli, Simone Speziale
Published: 2006-04-10
arXiv ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/gr-qc/0604044v2
Categories: gr-qc, hep-ph, hep-th
Abstract:
We compute some components of the graviton propagator in loop quantum gravity, using the spinfoam formalism, up to some second order terms in the expansion parameter.

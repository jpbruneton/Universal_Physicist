Title: Generalized Uncertainty Principle from Quantum Geometry
Authors: S. Capozziello, G. Lambiase, G. Scarpetta
Published: 1999-10-05
arXiv ID: http://arxiv.org/abs/gr-qc/9910017v1
Categories: gr-qc
Abstract:
The generalized uncertainty principle of string theory is derived in the framework of
 Quantum Geometry by taking into account the existence of an upper limit on the acceleration of massive particles.
